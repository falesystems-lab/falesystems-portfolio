## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Competition
**Threat:** Bigger players crush us
**Mitigation:**
- Niche focus (don't compete head-on with MEE6)
- Better free tier (convert users from competitors)
- Speed advantage (ship features faster)
- Personal brand (authentic, not corporate)

### Risk 2: Platform Dependency
**Threat:** Discord/Google changes algorithms
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify (multiple revenue streams)
- Own audience (email list, website)
- Multi-platform (Telegram, WhatsApp bots too)
- Terms compliance (never risk bans)

### Risk 3: Quality Issues
**Threat:** Bad product = bad reviews
**Mitigation:**
- Beta testing before launch
- Rapid bug fixes (24-hour response)
- Proactive monitoring (error tracking)
- Customer support priority

### Risk 4: Burnout
**Threat:** Too much manual work
**Mitigation:**
- Automation first (let systems run)
- Hire help at $2k/mo revenue (VA, support)
- Focus on high-leverage tasks only
- Heartbeat automation (I work while you sleep)
