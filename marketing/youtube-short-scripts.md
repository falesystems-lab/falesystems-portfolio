## Short #7: "Website Speed Hack (2s ‚Üí 0.5s)"

‚Üí Too slow, losing visitors

The fix: Lazy loading images
‚Üí Only load images when user scrolls to them
‚Üí Not when page loads

Code:

Results:
‚Üí 2 seconds ‚Üí 0.5 seconds

## Production Tips for Shorts

### Recording:
- **Phone camera** (vertical, 1080x1920)
- **OBS Studio** (for screen recordings)
- **CapCut** (for editing, free)

### Editing:
- **Fast cuts** (keep energy high)
- **Text overlays** (many watch without sound)
- **Transitions** (keep it smooth)
- **Music** (upbeat, copyright-free from YouTube Audio Library)

### Thumbnails:
- **Bright colors** (orange, yellow, red)
- **Faces** (if showing yourself)
- **Text** (3-5 words max)

### Posting Strategy:
- **Best times:** 12pm-3pm, 7pm-10pm (EST)
- **Frequency:** 1-2 per day minimum
- **Consistency:** Post daily for 30 days to test what works

### Growth Hacks:
- **Hook in first 3 seconds** (watch time matters)
- **End with question** (boosts comments)
- **Pin comment** with link to portfolio
- **Reply to all comments** (boosts engagement)
- **Use hashtags** (#webdev #coding #seo)
