## Content Template (What to Include)

### Comparison Post Structure:
```markdown
# [Title with Keyword]

## Introduction (100 words)
- Hook (problem)
- Promise (solution)
- Credibility (why trust us)

## Quick Comparison Table
[Feature comparison of top 5-7 options]

## Detailed Reviews
### Option 1: [Name]
- What it is
- Pros (3-5 bullets)
- Cons (2-3 bullets)
- Best for: [use-case]
- Pricing
- Screenshot

[Repeat for each option]

## How to Choose
- Criteria 1
- Criteria 2
- Criteria 3

## FAQ
[5-8 common questions]

## Conclusion
- Summary
- Top pick for most people
- CTA (try our tool / read more)
```

**Word count:** 2000-2500 words
**Images:** 5-10 screenshots/graphics
**Internal links:** 3-5
**External links:** 2-3 (authoritative sources)
