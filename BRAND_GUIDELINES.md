## üìù Typography

### Font Stack

**Primary (Web):**
```css
font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Inter', 'SF Pro', 'Segoe UI', 
             'Roboto', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
```

**Code/Monospace:**
```css
font-family: 'JetBrains Mono', 'Fira Code', 'Monaco', 'Courier New', monospace;
```

### Type Scale

**Display (Hero headlines):**
- Size: 48-72px (mobile: 32-48px)
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Line height: 1.1
- Use: Landing page heroes, major headings

**H1 (Page titles):**
- Size: 36-48px (mobile: 28-36px)
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Line height: 1.2

**H2 (Section headings):**
- Size: 28-36px (mobile: 24-28px)
- Weight: Semi-bold (600)
- Line height: 1.3

**H3 (Subsections):**
- Size: 20-24px (mobile: 18-20px)
- Weight: Semi-bold (600)
- Line height: 1.4

**Body (Paragraph text):**
- Size: 16-18px
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Line height: 1.6
- Max width: 70 characters

**Small (Captions, metadata):**
- Size: 14px
- Weight: Regular (400)
- Line height: 1.5

### Typography Rules

- **Never use all caps for body text** (hard to read)
- **Use sentence case for buttons** ("Get started" not "GET STARTED")
- **Max 3 font weights** (regular, semi-bold, bold)
- **Line length 50-70 characters** (optimal readability)

### Core Principles

**Direct:**
- ‚úÖ "Landing pages that convert"

**No Fluff:**
- ‚úÖ "Deployed in 24 hours"
- ‚ùå "We leverage our expertise to facilitate rapid deployment cycles"

**Confident:**
- ‚úÖ "I'll build it better"
- ‚ùå "I hope I can help with that"

**Technical (when relevant):**
- ‚úÖ "Next.js + Tailwind, deployed to Vercel"
- ‚ùå "Built with modern technologies"

**Human:**
- ‚úÖ "Let's build something"
- ‚ùå "We would be delighted to collaborate with you on your project"

### Content Guidelines

**Headlines:**
- Short (5-7 words)
- Benefit-focused
- Action-oriented

**Body Copy:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Active voice
- Second person ("you" not "one")

**CTAs:**
- Verb-first ("Get started" "See examples" "Start building")
- Clear outcome ("Get your landing page" not "Submit")
- No generic "Click here" or "Learn more"

### Photography

**DO:**
- Dark, moody backgrounds
- Code screenshots with syntax highlighting
- Real project examples
- High contrast

**DON'T:**
- Stock photos of people pointing at screens

- Small: 8px (0.5rem)
- Medium: 16px (1rem)
- Large: 32px (2rem)
- XL: 64px (4rem)

**Sections:**
- Vertical spacing: 80-120px
- Mobile: 40-60px
