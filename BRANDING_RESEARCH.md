### Typography Best Practices:

**Wordmark fonts:**
- Sans-serif (modern, clean)
- Bold or Semi-bold (authority)
- Custom spacing (unique feel)

**Body text:**
- 16-18px minimum (readability)
- 1.5-1.6 line-height (breathing room)
- Max 70 characters per line

**Headers:**
- 2x-4x body size
- Bold weight
- Short, punchy

---

### Profile/Landing Page Elements (Trust Signals):

**Must-haves for new brands:**

1. **Professional headshot or logo** (not generic avatar)
2. **Clear value proposition** (what do you do, in 10 words)
3. **Social proof** (testimonials, numbers, certifications)
4. **Portfolio/examples** (show, don't tell)
5. **Contact info** (email, response time)
6. **Pricing transparency** (no "contact for quote" bullshit)

**Fiverr/Upwork specifics:**
- 5-star rating system ‚Üí need testimonials fast
- Response time badge ‚Üí reply within 1 hour
- Portfolio images ‚Üí 3-5 best examples
- Certifications ‚Üí take platform tests
- Completion rate ‚Üí deliver on time, every time

## Competitive Analysis: Fiverr Top Sellers

### Common traits of 6-figure Fiverr sellers:

1. **Profile Photo:**
   - Professional (not selfie)
   - Smiling (approachable)
   - High quality (not pixelated)

2. **Gig Images:**
   - Custom graphics (not stock photos)
   - Show process or results
   - Branded (consistent colors/fonts)
   - Text overlays (value prop)

3. **Description:**
   - Bullet points (scannable)
   - FAQ section (preempt questions)
   - Clear deliverables (no ambiguity)
   - Fast response promise

4. **Pricing:**
   - 3 tiers (psychological anchoring)
   - Clear differentiation (basic, standard, premium)
   - Add-ons for upsells

5. **Reviews:**
   - 100+ five-stars (social proof)
   - Response to ALL reviews (engagement)
   - Detailed testimonials (not just "great!")
