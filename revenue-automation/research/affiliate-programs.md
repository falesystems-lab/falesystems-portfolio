## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Research
**Tools:** Ahrefs (or free alternatives: Ubersuggest, Google Keyword Planner)

**Target Criteria:**
- Search volume: 500-5000/mo (sweet spot)
- Difficulty: <30 (we can rank)
- CPC: $2+ (commercial intent)
- Long-tail: 3-5 words

**Example Targets:**
- "best vps hosting for discord bot" (KD 15, Vol 800)
- "jasper ai alternative free" (KD 20, Vol 1200)
- "shopify vs woocommerce 2026" (KD 25, Vol 3000)

### Content Production
**Volume:** 3-5 articles/week = 200+ articles/year

**Quality:**
- 1500-2500 words (sweet spot for ranking)
- AI-written + human edited (I write, you review)
- Original images/screenshots
- Comparison tables
- FAQ sections (featured snippets)

**Internal Linking:**
- Each post links to 3-5 other posts
- Hub pages for each category
- Silo structure (developer tools → hosting → VPS)

### Backlink Strategy
**Goal:** 50+ quality backlinks in first 6 months

**Tactics:**
1. **Guest Posts:** Offer to write for related blogs (free content for them, backlink for us)
2. **Resource Pages:** Find "[niche] tools" lists, ask to be included
3. **HARO:** Help a Reporter Out (journalist quotes → backlinks)
4. **Broken Link Building:** Find dead links, offer our content as replacement
5. **Community:** Reddit, forums (non-spammy participation)

## Conversion Optimization

### Landing Page Elements:
- **Comparison Tables:** Visual, easy to scan
- **Pros/Cons:** Balanced, trustworthy
- **Screenshots:** Show the actual tool
- **Pricing:** Transparent (vs affiliate link surprise)
- **CTA:** "Get Started", "Try Free", "Claim Discount"

### A/B Testing:
- Button text: "Get Started" vs "Try Free"
- Button color: Blue vs Orange
- Placement: Top vs bottom vs both
- Discount emphasis: "Save 20%" vs "Limited Time"

### Trust Signals:
- "Last updated: [date]" (freshness)
- "We may earn commission" (disclosure)
- Author bio (expertise)
- Comments/social proof
